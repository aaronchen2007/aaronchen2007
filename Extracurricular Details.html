Extracurricular Activities

Model United Nations

I have participated in three Kang Chiao Model United Nations Conferences. The three committees I was in had agendas including regulating GM crops, establishing protection for women facing gender-based violence in the Middle East, and regulating nuclear waste disposal from industrial production.

NFTE World Series of Innovation Challenge

Three classmates and I competed in the 2023 NFTE World Series of Innovation Aramco Connected Cities Challenge. We proposed a business model for establishing a network of infrared sensors attached to traffic lights to track traffic flow, adjust the traffic light waiting time, and utilize the data for AI model training to increase traffic efficiency and sustainability.

Volunteer at Boyo Social Welfare Foundation for Learning Supporting Courses for Elementary School Students

From 2022 to 2024, as a member of the Kang Chiao Service Club, I was a volunteer at Boyo Social Welfare Foundation. My fellow club members and I taught elementary school students from a remote area in Hsinchu English, math, and reading. I also became the vice club leader in 2024.
